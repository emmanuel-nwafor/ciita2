Catholic Institute of Information and Technololgy


Welcome to the Catholic Institute of Information and Technology Awgu, a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality education and training in
computer science and information technology, rooted in Catholic values and principles.

Why Choose Us

Cause We Are The Best




At CIITA, we are a community of faithful individuals dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our students and society. Our faculty and staff are experienced professionals with a deep understanding of the tech industry and its evolving needs.


We are empowered by the teachings of Christ, we aim to educate and train individuals to become competent, compassionate, and ethical professionals in the field of information technology, serving the Church and society.


Catholic Institute of Information and Technology Awgu was founded by the Catholic Diocese of Awgu to address the growing need for IT professionals who share the values of the Catholic Church.
